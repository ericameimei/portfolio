About

Massachusetts-based artist & community leader.

I work primarily in oil pastels and paint, with a practice centered around movement, humor, and storytelling—often inspired by animals, everyday moments, and the emotional texture of memory.

Alongside my personal practice, I build creative spaces that make art feel accessible, social, and fun rather than intimidating or precious.
